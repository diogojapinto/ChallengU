Describe your app

This application envisions a dynamic platform where members interact based on the premise of exchanging challenges. These challenges, as the main feature, are surrounded by a rich environment which integrates numerous features, many of them derive from gamification.

After registering on the platform, the user is able to import his/her friend list from Facebook, and send to them an invitation to also join the application. Then, the registered user is able to create challenges if they have enough "challenge points" to do so (if they do, they will be decremented as consequence of this action). If they do, they can target the audience for it: all community, friends or a single person (in which case the challenge can be made private). The recently created challenge is evaluated by the challenger and by community, awarding “experience points” to the ones who prove that they have completed it. This “proof” can take on the form of text, sound, picture or video, depending also on the nature of the challenge, and it is then evaluated by the community.

The challenge points are, in essence, the fuel of the application. If the users do not interact sufficiently with it, they do not earn challenge points and so they are inhibited to post new challenges. In order to prevent the platform from reaching an halt, in addition to being awarded challenge points by completing challenges, the users will also earn some in a weekly basis, just by being registered. This will enable us to keep a dynamic and rich flux of interactions within our application. Each challenge completed awards experience points, allowing the user to continuously level up. Through numerous other interactions with the platform, the user may also earn achievements, which he/she can display on his/her profile. The exchange of user's profiles, filled with this information, will keep them engaged and foment the coopetition within the application.

As a obvious side-note, the whole application will be supervised by moderators, capable of deleting comments, challenges and responses to challenges, warn users about misbehavior and of other abilities that enables them to keep a clean and fruitful application. These moderators may be elected from the root Administrator account.

Alongside these features, some others may be found suited to be implemented during the development of the application, with the vision of fomenting interaction, fun and sometimes even provide the opportunity for people to become better and to feel better with themselves.
